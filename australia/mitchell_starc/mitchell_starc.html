Role: Left-arm Fast Bowler

Bio: Mitchell Starc is one of the fastest bowlers in world cricket, known for his devastating yorkers and consistent ability to take wickets at crucial moments. He has been a key player for Australia in all formats, especially in limited-overs cricket.
