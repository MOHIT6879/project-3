James Anderson

Role: Fast Bowler

Bio: James Anderson is one of the greatest fast bowlers in cricket history. Renowned for his swing bowling, he has been a pivotal figure in the England bowling attack since his debut. Anderson's longevity and performance have made him a leading wicket-taker in Test cricket.
